Difficulty: Easy | Time: 1 hour

Author: VeggieChef00

About

A delectable vegetarian version of the timeless lasagna, brimming with wholesome ingredients and robust flavors. Immerse yourself in the essence of wholesome goodness as mushrooms, spinach, and zucchini come together in a flavorful dance. This vegetarian rendition is a celebration of nature's bounty, offering a rich and fulfilling dining experience for both the seasoned herbivore and the curious food enthusiast.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Assorted vegetables for filling (e.g., carrots, spinach, zucchini)
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmigiano Reggiano

Instructions

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Prepare the vegetable filling in a pan.
  3. Layer the lasagna in a baking dish with tomato sauce, noodles, vegetable filling, and mozzarella.
  4. Repeat the layers and sprinkle Parmigiano Reggiano on top.
  5. Bake in the oven until golden and bubbly.

Enjoy this delightful vegetarian lasagna!
